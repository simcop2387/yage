class Scene : yage.node.basenode.BaseNode;
A Scene is the root of a tree of Nodes, and obviously, a Scene never has a parent. Certain "global" variables are stored per Scene and affect all Nodes in that Scene . Examples include global ambient lighting and the speed of sound. Scenes also maintain a Horde (array) of all lights in them.
